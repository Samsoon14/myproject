About Overthinkers

Overthinkers are people who think too much about everything.

What is Overthinking?

Overthinking is a state where a person keeps thinking about the same thing over and over again.

The Overthinker's Mind

An overthinker's mind is always active. They think about every aspect of every situation.

How to Understand Overthinking?

To understand overthinking, it is important to first observe your own thoughts.
