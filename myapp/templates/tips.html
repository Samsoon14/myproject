Tips to Control Overthinking

1. Write Your Thoughts

Whenever you overthink, write your thoughts on paper. This reduces mental load.

2. Deep Breathing

Breathe in for 5 seconds, hold for 5 seconds, release for 5 seconds. This reduces anxiety.

3. Stay in the Present

Only think about now. Let go of worries about the past and future.

4. Exercise

Walk or exercise for 30 minutes daily. It keeps your mind fresh.

5. Talk to Someone

Share your thoughts with a friend or family member. Don't think alone.
